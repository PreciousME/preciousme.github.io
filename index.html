ABOUT ME

I'm a human driven by relentless curiosity. Asking the question "WHY?" is my superpower. My journey into design started by questioning things, growing up watching my relation building apps, I always had something to question. That's how it all began.

I believe design is just more than how it works, but most importantly – how it impacts us. This mentality enables me to offer my best in any work I find myself. I enjoy exploring various ways to solve problems and being involved in the whole process of following up ideas from concept to execution through continuous iteration and testing by leveraging the power of design thinking, technology, and business.

I'm particular about the business side of what I'm working on, as well as how it intersects with technology. I code HTML/CSS while exploring how technology interfaces with design and this knowledge affect how I think about solutions, knowing what is technologically feasible.

I've seen the power of collaboration, research and putting people at the center of any endeavor. And so, whether it's a small project I'm working on or a big thing, I bring people into the picture while working tirelessly to gain full understanding of people and whatever it is we are working on.

I'm an ardent reader of books, articles and I watch videos that interest me. I also find pleasure in writing. I find time to meditate, engage in physical activities while ensuring that I have enough rest. The beach is my favorite place.

I'm currently leading design at Advance.
Consulting, working with agencies and startups, I’ve had the opportunity to lead teams while working on projects for businesses like Google, Zenith Bank, GTBank, MNET Africa, Planned Parenthood, Helium; to mention but a few.
